How We Work

From briefing to day one

A disciplined five-step search process, refined for technical and engineering markets.

Discover

We start with your business, not the job spec — the project, the team, the growth plan and what success in the role really looks like at six and eighteen months.

Search

A targeted campaign across our sector networks: direct approaches, referrals and market mapping. You get visibility of the market as we work it.

Vet

In-depth interviews assessing technical depth, motivations and cultural alignment. Only candidates who pass every gate — and genuinely want the move — go forward.

Present

A concise shortlist with honest, detailed profiles — strengths, considerations, salary expectations and availability. No padding, no CV spam.

Support

Interview coordination, offer management and resignation support, then structured check-ins through onboarding. We stay involved until the hire has stuck.
