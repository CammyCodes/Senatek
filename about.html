About Senatek

Built to power tomorrow's workforce

A specialist consultancy founded on a simple belief: the infrastructure that powers our future deserves better than transactional recruitment.

Our Story

Specialists by choice

Senatek Recruitment was founded in 2025 by Jordan Camm to serve the sectors driving the UK and Europe's biggest engineering challenge: building, powering and maintaining the infrastructure of tomorrow. From grid upgrades and data centre expansion to the race for renewable capacity, demand for exceptional technical talent has never been higher — and the cost of a wrong hire never greater.

We work exclusively in power & energy, critical infrastructure & data centres, building services & facilities, and renewables & energy storage. That focus is deliberate. It means we know the projects, the salary benchmarks, the qualifications that matter and — most importantly — the people.

Every search is delivered personally, from first briefing to first day and beyond. No hand-offs, no juniors learning on your time. Just a genuine talent partner invested in the outcome.

Leadership

Meet the founder

Jordan Camm

Founder & Director

Jordan founded Senatek after seeing first-hand how the engineering and infrastructure sectors were being let down by volume-driven recruitment. Working across the built environment and energy markets, one thing became clear: the businesses delivering the UK and Europe's most critical projects need a partner who understands them — not another agency chasing placements.

Today Jordan works directly with leadership teams across all four of Senatek's sectors, personally managing every search from briefing to offer. Clients get senior-level market insight; candidates get honest, confidential career guidance from someone who knows their world.

"Behind every project powering tomorrow, there's a person who made it happen. My job is finding them."
